# SKILL.md — Weekly Review

**Trigger:** "weekly review" / "week review" / end of week / start of new week

**Purpose:** Strategic audit — zoom out, close loose ends, plan ahead.

---

## Instructions

### 1. Collect (5 min)
- Review `memory/` files from the past week — what actually happened?
- Scan `INBOX.md` — anything missed?
- Check `WAITING_FOR.md` — anything to chase?

### 2. Process (10 min)
- Clear the INBOX — triage every item
- Update `projects/INDEX.md` — accurate statuses
- Move completed items to archive in relevant project files
- Chase anything overdue in `WAITING_FOR.md`

### 3. Review (10 min)
- `GOALS.md` — are you making progress? Drifting?
- `DECISIONS.md` — any decisions that need revisiting?
- `SOMEDAY.md` — anything ready to promote to active?
- `RECURRING.md` — anything missed this week?

### 4. Plan (10 min)
- What are the 3 most important things next week?
- Any meetings to prep for?
- Any blockers to clear?

### 5. Close with a summary:
```
## Weekly Review — Week of [date]

### Done this week
-

### Carried forward
-

### Focus next week
1.
2.
3.

### Notes / Reflections
-
```
Add this summary to `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md` (Friday or Monday date).

---

## Notes
- Aim for 30 min max. Timebox ruthlessly.
- The goal is clarity, not perfection.
